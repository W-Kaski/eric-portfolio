A collaborative, AI-powered cloud gallery platform designed for teams and creative professionals.

## Core Capabilities
- **Collaborative Workspaces:** Built team spaces supporting real-time photo sharing, collaborative editing, and permission management.
- **AI Image Processing:** Integrated AI vision capabilities for automated image tagging, optimization, and AI-assisted generative edits directly in the browser.
- **Domain-Driven Design (DDD):** Refactored the core monolithic services using Domain-Driven Design principles to handle complex business logic relating to image metadata, storage limits, and team roles.
- **Scalable Architecture:** Implemented chunked uploading for massive files, background processing for image compression, and analytics dashboards for image interaction metrics.
