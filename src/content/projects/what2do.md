A comprehensive community-driven events and club discovery platform built exclusively for the University of Toronto Mississauga (UTM) campus.

## Platform Features
- **Interactive Campus Map:** Real-time spatial discovery of ongoing events using map-centric navigation anchored to exact building coordinates (e.g., IB, DV).
- **Club Ecosystem:** Role-based access control (RBAC) allowing Club Managers to create events, moderate members, and maintain their public profiles.
- **Feed & Engagement:** A social feed supporting image/video posts with full engagement mechanics (likes, nested comments, reposts) and full-text content searching.
- **Performance Optimized Data Model:** Engineered a scalable PostgreSQL schema supporting advanced geospatial queries and materialized view counts for "Hot Events" and trending tags.
